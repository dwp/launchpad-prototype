Upload evidence to support your PIP claim

It's important you provide evidence or information about the impact of any health conditions or disabilities on your daily life, if you can.

Uploaded evidence may mean:

  • we can make a quicker decision on your claim
  • we can make an accurate decision and pay you the right amount
  • you may not need to attend an assessment with a health professional

You should only upload photos or scanned images of documents you already have. These documents should show how your health condition or disability affects you.

Do not request documents you might have to pay for, for example a letter from your GP. If we need this evidence we can ask for it ourselves, as long as we have your permission.
