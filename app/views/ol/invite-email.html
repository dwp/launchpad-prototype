Dear Jamie,

We have received the Personal Independence Payment (PIP) application you have started.

Next, we will ask about your daily living and mobility needs to help us make our decision.

If we need more information after you have submitted the application we may invite you to an assessment.

When to complete your application by

You will need to answer all the questions and upload any evidence you have by:

30 September 2025

If you need more time

Contact us if you need more time to complete this application. Our contract details are at the end of this email.

If you have not submitted the application by the return date, and you have not let us know, we may end the PIP application.

Disability Living Allowance

Disability Living Allowance (DLA) is being replaced by PIP. If you are currently receiving DLA this will stop if you do not complete the form by the retutn date or contact us for more time.

How to get started

You will need a GOV.UK One Login to use this service. You will use this to log in to your account in the future

If you do not have a GOV.UK One Login, you will be asked to create one when you start your PIP application.

To create your GOV.UK One Login, go to: https://www.apply-for-pip.dwp.gov.uk/

You will need your:

  • email address and access to your emails
  • mobile phone, tablet or desktop computer

Help and support

There is information at each step of the online service to help you.
You can download our PDF guide to complete the form once you have registered.

If you change your mind and would prefer to complete a paper form instead, contact us so we can post it to you.
Changes you need to tell us about

You must tell us about any changes to your circumstances by post or telephone.

You can find out more about the types of changes that we need to know about by visiting https://www.gov.uk/pip/change-of-circumstances.

How to contact us

You can contact us by:

  • Telephone: 0800 121 4433
  • Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 121 4433

Other ways to communicate with us

If you need braille, British Sign Language, a hearing loop, translations, large print, Easy Read, audio, or something else, please contact us.
If you use Relay UK dial 18001 followed by our telephone number
