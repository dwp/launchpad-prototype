Prove your identity with a GOV.UK One Login

You need to prove your identity with GOV.UK One Login to finish your application.

This means you will not need to prove your identity again next time a GOV.UK One Login service needs to check who you are.

You can prove your identity online if you have a:

  • UK passport
  • driving licence
  • other form of accepted photo ID

You can prove your identity at a Post Office if you have a:

  • EU driving licence
  • non-UK passport
