Disability Living Allowance

Disability Living Allowance (DLA) is being replaced by PIP. If they are currently receiving DLA, this will stop if you do not complete the form by the return date or contact us for more time.


How to get started

Create your account

To register for our online services, you will need your:

  • email address and access to your emails
  • mobile phone

The first time you sign in, you will be asked to enter some personal details so we can match them to our records.


Complete your identity check

Before continuing the application, we will ask you to confirm your identity. This is to protect you against fraud.
We will guide you through what you need to do at each step, but it will be useful to have these ready:

  • Your National Insurance number
  • 2 types of identity documentation

Once successfully completed, you can continue the application.

Register to complete your application

Create an account to continue the PIP application.

If you already have an account, you can sign in.

http://apply-for-pip.dwp.gov.uk/home

Useful tips before you start

It might be helpful to keep a diary for a few days, you can note down:

  • how their health condition or disability affects their daily life
  • a list of things they need help with or find difficult

You can also gather information to help you fill in the form and it can be uploaded as evidence to support your answers.

You do not need to answer all the questions in one go. You can save the application and return to it later by signing back in.

Help and support

There is information at each step of the online service to help you.

You can download our PDF guide to complete the form once you have registered.

If you change your mind and would prefer to complete a paper form instead, contact us so we can post it to you.

Changes you need to tell us about

You must tell us about any changes to their circumstances by post or telephone.
You can find out more about the types of changes that we need to know about by visiting www.gov.uk/pip/change-of-circumstances.
