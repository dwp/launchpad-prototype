What to upload

Do upload

Examples of documents you could upload include:

  • care or treatment plans
  • information from your consultants about your condition or diagnosis
  • test results
  • a repeat prescription list
  • a statement or diary from a carer or family member

Do not upload

Do not upload:

  • physical proof of you condition or disability. For example a photo of your prosthetic limb
  • appointment cards or letters
  • general information or fact sheets about your condition or medication

You can add more evidence afer you've submitted your PIP claim if you need to.
